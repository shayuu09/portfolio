Poster Design

Graphic design is my passion.
Click here for fullscreen!

Graphic design is something I quite like doing. This specific design is for a GBDA 101 in-class activity, where we designed posters for random verbs. I find my form of poster design tends to incorporate illustrative and smooth elements. In terms of methods, I also enjoy using noise and texture on posters.

One specific site I like using is called tooooools. It's capable of turning images into interesting graphics to use in posters. The textures available are super fun and align perfectly with my graphical style.
