Band T-shirt Merch

For Foxgloves
Click here, here, and here for fullscreens!

One of my dearest friends, Daniel Bezerra, is the man behind the musician Foxgloves. His music is reminiscent of those like American Football and honestly I think he's not gotten the recognition he deserves. Recently he reached out to me to design merch for his music and I could not have been more glad to.

The first iteration is a nod to the style of show fliers he usually posts, with a gothic font and dotted black animal graphics. The shirt is similar, sporting a foxglove on the front and the lyrics to his song i heard the owen sound.

The second iteration utilizes a hand-drawn feel, featuring a fox named Gimlet. The shirt is similarly simple, with a front graphic and nothing on its back.

However, Dan's favourite seems to be the third iteration, which is similar to the first in its fonts. However, this time it's paired images with white graphics. The shirt design uses the cover art to his song Vestigial Trace (also drawn by me) and the initials "VT" on the front.

My personal favourite is probably the first iteration, but the third iteration is a close second. I had a wonderful time designing each of these iterations, and would love to be able to do more complex designs (if Dan's merchandising budget allows it). Until then, we stick with graphic tees and sweats like it's the 90's!
